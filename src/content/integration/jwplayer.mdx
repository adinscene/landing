#### How to integrate Adinscene with JW Player?

JW Player provides enterprise-grade video delivery with built-in monetization tools. Our integration enhances their platform with AI-powered scene recognition and contextual ad targeting capabilities.

1. Enable Adinscene in your JW Player dashboard
2. Configure scene-based ad rules and frequency caps
3. Set up contextual targeting parameters
4. Track performance with enhanced analytics

#### Features available in the Integration

- **Server-Side Ad Insertion**: Seamless ad delivery without buffering
- **Adaptive Bitrate**: Automatic quality adjustment for smooth playback
- **DRM Support**: Protect premium content with industry-standard encryption
- **Live & VOD**: Support for both live streams and on-demand content
- **Advanced Analytics**: Deep insights into viewer behavior and ad performance
