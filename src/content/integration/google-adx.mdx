#### How to integrate Adinscene with Google Ad Exchange?

Google Ad Exchange (AdX) provides access to the world's largest pool of advertisers. Our integration enriches your inventory with scene and transcript metadata to achieve higher CPMs through contextual relevance.

1. Link your Google Ad Manager account
2. Configure floor prices based on content categories
3. Enable scene-based targeting for premium inventory
4. Optimize yield with our AI-powered recommendations

#### Features available in the Integration

- **Real-Time Bidding**: Maximize revenue with competitive auctions
- **Global Demand**: Access to millions of advertisers worldwide
- **Dynamic Pricing**: AI-optimized floor prices based on content value
- **Header Bidding**: Compete with multiple demand sources simultaneously
- **Comprehensive Reporting**: Detailed insights into revenue performance
